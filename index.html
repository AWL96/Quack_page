About

Hi there,

You're most likely visiting this page because you've encountered its URL in my probes that you investigated. The reason for these probes is because I'm currently working on a research project where I'm measuring the reachability of middleboxes (i.a. within your network). To this end, I'm sending packets with a very small HTTP GET request to various IP addresses and observe the responses. To limit the impact on the network/end-hosts, the experiments are spaced out over several days and are performed at a low bandwidth.
